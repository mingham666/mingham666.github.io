About Me

If you view the sites I have created, you will see proof of my earlier statements, expecially about my attention to detail. I've enjoyed my time at University, but what I'm really looking forward to is graduating so I may further build upon the knowledge I've gained at university and apply it at the workplace (again, sorry the cliche line, but it's true!). I'd love for you to contact me should you have any questions, please also let me know if you find any strange bugs on this site, I'm always improving!

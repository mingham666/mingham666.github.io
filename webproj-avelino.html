Avelino Media

Avelino is an interesting one, some friends of a friend wanted to hire me (and actually pay me!) to do them a professional looking site to promote their business in increasing internet traffic/presence for small and medium sized businesses. Naturally, I jumped at the idea, they already had decent connections from word of mouth, but wanted a modern and sleek site to refer potential customers to. Avelino features a parallax scrolling effect for the slides on desktop broswers, and a clean, no-nonsense presentation throughout.

-Return to Projects-

What I Did

This site included a lot of custom images being created. I designed and created the logo myself, and all of the background images, such as the town silhouette and the global connections image. I picked out colours that would imitate the sun setting over the town as the page scrolled, and generally went for dark blues, purples and blacks. The site scales well for mobile devices as to be expected, and doesn’t sacrifice any usability when on such devices aside from some small niggles with the parallax effect, depending of the phone and/or browser. CSS and HTML was done from scratch, and I will be building a contact form and an area for the admin to login to view messages in the very near future.

Why I Did It

I won’t take on a project if it doesn’t interest me personally from a design perspective, or if I don’t believe the idea will work. Fortunately, Avelino Media has a strong potential customer base which will only benefit leaps and bounds by having an online reference point. This was a fun project that took a bit longer than I expected it to, considering the amount of content on the site so far, but all in all it was a relatively painless experience in the grand scheme of things.

Who I Did It For

As mentioned previously, Avelino Media is comprised of two friends who have a vicious desire to succeed. With their entrepreneurial spirit, Avelino is sure to thrive online, and I look forward to working with them on future projects.
